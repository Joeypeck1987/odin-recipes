Christmas Eggnog

Creamy delicious homemade eggnog will have friends and family begging for more. This recipe calls for cooked eggs for safety, and this is also alcohol free. 'Tis the season.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • 12 large egg yolks
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 4 cups light cream
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions

  1. Combine milk, cloves, cinnamon, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla in a saucepan. Heat over lowest setting for 5 minutes, then increase heat and slowly bring to a boil.
  2. Whisk together egg yolks and sugar in a large bowl until fluffy. Whisk hot milk mixture slowly into egg yolk mixture. Pour mixture back into the saucepan.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick, about 3 minutes; do not allow mixture to boil. Strain to remove cloves and let cool for about 1 hour.
  4. Stir in cream, rum, remaining 2 teaspoons vanilla, and nutmeg. Refrigerate before serving, 8 hours to overnight.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:456 calories; protein 7.5g; carbohydrates 32.6g; fat 21.5g; cholesterol 264.2mg; sodium 73.9mg.
